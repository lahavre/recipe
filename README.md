# Recipe Box

A personal recipe website — plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, no build step required.

Live site: https://lahavre.github.io/recipe/

## Browsing

The home page has two expandable sections, **Foods** and **Drinks**. Click one to reveal its subcategories:

- **Foods** — Western, Italian, Chinese, Japanese
- **Drinks** — Fruit Tea, Tea, Coffee

Click a subcategory to see its recipes, and click a recipe to open the full page (ingredients scale to your serving size, and cooking steps have built-in timers).
