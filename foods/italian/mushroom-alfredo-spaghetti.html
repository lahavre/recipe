Mushroom Alfredo Spaghetti

Creamy garlic parmesan alfredo sauce with sautéed mushrooms tossed through spaghetti.

Prep
10 min

    Method

      Notes

      Brown the mushrooms well before adding garlic — crowding the pan steams them instead. For extra depth, deglaze with a splash of white wine after the garlic. Add cooked chicken or crispy bacon to make it heartier. A sweated shallot or half an onion before the garlic adds gentle depth if you'd like it.
