Sicilian Stuffed Eggplant

Baked eggplant halves stuffed with a quick garlic-basil tomato sauce, mozzarella, and black olives — Sicilian sunshine on a plate.

10 min
Cook

    Method

      Notes

      Score the flesh in a diamond pattern without piercing the skin — it helps the eggplant cook evenly and makes it easy to mash into a well later, while the skin stays intact as a shell.

      The dish rewards a little patience: letting the tomato sauce reduce properly and the eggplant fully soften before stuffing is what brings the flavours together.

      Buffalo mozzarella is traditional here, but a ball of fior di latte works well too if that's what you have on hand.

      Best served warm, straight from the oven, as a light main or a side.
