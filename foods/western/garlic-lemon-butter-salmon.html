Pan-Grilled Salmon with Garlic Lemon Butter

Crispy-seared fillets bathed in a glossy garlic-lemon butter sauce, spooned over the top right in the pan.

    Method

      Notes

      Pat the salmon bone-dry before searing or it will steam instead of crisp, and leave it undisturbed once it hits the pan so the skin releases on its own. Pull it at 49–52°C internal for medium — it keeps cooking off the heat. Pairs beautifully with rice, roasted asparagus, or a simple green salad.
