Creamy Mushroom Soup

A silky, deeply savory mushroom soup with no blending required — a mix of finely chopped and sliced mushrooms gives it body, and a simple roux ties it all together.

      Notes

      No blender needed: finely chopping half the mushrooms and leaving the rest sliced gives the soup a naturally thick, chunky-creamy texture as it simmers.

      This soup is thickened with a flour-based roux rather than a purée, so it's best made fresh — it can turn a little stodgy after freezing and thawing. It keeps well refrigerated for up to 3 days.

      For a dairy-free version, swap the milk for oat or almond milk.

      A mix of mushroom varieties (portobello, cremini, shiitake) adds extra depth, but any one type works well on its own too.
